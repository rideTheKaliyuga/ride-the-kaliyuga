My Traces

By Harsh Patel | Published: November 26, 2024

The leaves rustling, the water flowing
They are all made of me; dull and glowing

I create all motion. Not with soil, but with emotion.
You are also my creation. Made up of the same potion.

Me within you keeps seeking
For me around you
Why hasn't the light of my answers found you?

Like your shadow with you, my companionship is true
Is it sunset! So what? I'm always on the journey with you

In the dark my trace; maybe invisible but unphased
I'm within your footsteps, however lost you're in the maze.

Me within you keeps seeking
For me around you
Why hasn't the light of my answers found you?

The destinies are my making; isn't the picture breathtaking?
One's trace disappears; our together is unshaking.

With your eyes on sight, around all this light!
Why seek questions within the answers you find?

Me within you keeps seeking
For me around you
Why hasn't the light of my answers found you?
