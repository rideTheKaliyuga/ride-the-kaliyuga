Some Beautiful Things

By Harsh Patel | Published: May 14, 2025

And so the sun shines
Like the color of your eyes
My mind wandered
You caught me by surprise

I was filled with joy
Like a 9-year old boy
Smiled and giggled
Eyes restlessly on that toy

The apple of my eye; the comfort of my soul
Some beautiful things lie; just out of my control

Do I love you? Yes!
Every moment I breathe
And even more so when
My heart skips a beat

You’re the colors of a rainbow
I’m the clouds around
I like floating in the sky
You keep kissing the ground

The apple of my eye; the comfort of my soul
Some beautiful things lie; just out of my control

Skipped beats hurt sharp
Feels like the toy’s not mine
And the sun doesn’t shine
No rainbow left to find

So today I declare
My-self to be dead
Like a fish out of ocean
In the sand; with the shells

The apple of my eye; the comfort of my soul
Some beautiful things lie, just out of my control

Puddles of tears; and a darkness divine
Prized possessions; in that chest of mine

The apple of my eye; the comfort of my soul
Some beautiful things lie, just out of my control
