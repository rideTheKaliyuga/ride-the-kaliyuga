All I Mined

By Harsh Patel | Published: July 5, 2025

Ding… ding… ding… Starts with adventure
Choices aplenty; feelings tender
In search of the truth, and everything better
Pump up the blood, I’m ready to gamble

Hoping that… I’m not blind
Conscious attention, focused eyes
Not a bit scared, to open up my heart
The fight is on - UNKNOWN v/ I.

Controlled breathing, in a tough fight
Meticulous work; each day, each night
Locked on a path, but something’s not right!
I hit my goals. But where’s my delight?

I question now… Am I really blind?
Or did I choose; to close my EYE?
Too scared to open up now
The consequence shall be faced by I!

Suckerpunched! Right in my chest
Did everything right, yet one regret.
I have to smile; I have to accept
my fate, as I walk the path to death.

So I learn… that I am blind
But what I closed was not my eye
And now it opens up in a beat
 Ding… ding… ding…
The consequence is all that’s mine.
